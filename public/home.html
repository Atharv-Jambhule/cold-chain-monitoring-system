Cold Chain Monitoring System

Ensure freshness and safety during storage and transportation of perishable goods with real-time IoT monitoring.

Why Cold Chain Monitoring?

Temperature-sensitive goods like dairy, vaccines, seafood, and fresh produce require controlled environments. This system uses IoT sensors to track data in real-time, prevent spoilage, ensure compliance, and reduce financial losses across the supply chain.
